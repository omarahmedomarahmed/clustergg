## Slide 1 — Title
- **Cluster** — Every game. One identity.
- clustergg.com
- Preseed round · $30,000
- **Visual:** the cosmic planet-globe hero + wordmark logo.

## Slide 2 — The problem
- A gamer's identity is scattered across 10+ platforms (Riot, Steam, Epic, Xbox, PlayStation, Chess.com, mobile…).
- No single credible place proves _"this is me across every game I play."_
- Discord communities have the audience — but no native way to show stats, run cross-game competitions, or monetize.
- **Visual:** a messy grid of disconnected game logos → one Cluster profile.

## Slide 3 — The solution
- One shareable profile that links **every** game account.
- Real, **API-verified** stats, ranks and trophies — not self-reported.
- Compete in **Challenges**, climb **Leaderboards**, progress through **Quests** (Cluster Points).
- **Visual:** `/u/{slug}` profile + a planet page.

## Slide 4 — Product tour
- **Planets:** each game is an interactive globe with its community, challenges and leaderboards.
- **Quests:** cosmic treasure-maps that gamify everything you do (Bronze → Platinum).
- **Challenges:** time-boxed, real-data competitions with live standings + countdowns.
- **Leaderboards:** per-game, API-verified, side by side.
- **Visual:** 4 screenshots (planet hero, quest map, challenge page, leaderboards page).

## Slide 5 — Why now
- Creator/gamer identity is going cross-platform; every provider now exposes stats APIs.
- Discord is the default gamer home (200M+ MAU) and is opening monetization.
- AI art + serverless infra make a polished, art-heavy platform buildable by a tiny team.

## Slide 6 — Traction / status
- **MVP is production-ready** and security-audited.
- **24 game providers integrated** (adapters shipped) — real partners below.
- A **500-game catalog** vision; growing early community.
- **Pre-revenue** on brand ads by design — we prioritized product + integrations first.
- **Visual:** logo wall of integrated games.

## Slide 7 — Our partners (integrations)
- Live API integrations: **Chess.com, Lichess, Dota 2, Speedrun.com, Roblox, Steam, League of Legends, VALORANT, Fortnite, Minecraft (Hypixel), PUBG, CS2 (FACEIT), Clash of Clans, Clash Royale, Brawl Stars, Xbox, osu!, Apex Legends, Battle.net, Epic Games, Discord, Mobile Legends, PlayStation, Call of Duty.**
- Each is a real provider adapter with a verified metric set.
- **Visual:** grid of the 24 provider logos labeled "Integrated."

## Slide 8 — Business model
- **Brand ads** — banner + 5s video placements across planets, feed, leaderboards and profiles. Full offline-sales admin **and a self-serve brand portal** are already built: brands get a key-gated dashboard with live analytics and upload their own creatives.
- **Discord ad network (next):** let Discord server owners **monetize their members** by running our sponsored placements where our bot lives; revenue share with servers.
- **Future:** premium gamer profiles, tournament tooling, sponsor challenges.

## Slide 9 — The Discord growth + monetization engine (what we build with the round)
- **Cluster bot** on Discord servers = organic user acquisition at near-zero CAC.
- Members get the **feed control panel, on Discord**: subscribe to leaderboards, get stat updates, see & join challenges, and track their progress — without leaving Discord.
- Server owners opt in to **sponsored ads** to their members → new revenue for them, ad inventory for us.
- Every bot interaction funnels gamers back to their Cluster profile.

## Slide 10 — Go-to-market
1. Seed the Discord bot into gaming servers (partnerships + organic).
2. Convert server members into Cluster profiles via stat tracking + challenges.
3. Turn profiles into recurring engagement via Quests + leaderboards.
4. Layer brand + server-network ads once audience density is proven.

## Slide 11 — The ask: $30,000 preseed
- **Use of funds (MVP → real production + growth):**
  - **Go-to-market execution** — Discord server partnerships, content, community.
  - **Discord bot build** — stat tracking, user search, leaderboard/challenge subscriptions, sponsored-ad delivery + server revenue share.
  - **Infra + API costs** — Neon, Vercel, provider API tiers, media/CDN.
  - **Art + polish** — planet/quest art pipeline at catalog scale.
- Runway to prove the Discord acquisition loop and switch on ad revenue.

## Slide 12 — Why us / close
- A shipped, production-ready MVP with 24 real integrations and a full admin CMS — built lean.
- Clear, low-CAC growth loop through Discord.
